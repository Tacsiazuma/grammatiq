FAQ

Does it use our own data?

Yes. Data is isolated with controlled access. We do not train public models on your content.

How is this different from an AI chat?

It’s a source‑ and style‑driven workflow with approvals and multi‑format outputs, not a free‑form chat.

Do we get a source list?

Yes. Every piece includes an automatic reference list with links and timestamps.

Can we load our brandbook/style guide?

Yes. The agent enforces terminology, banned terms, tone, and examples as rules.

What integrations are supported?

Google Docs, Notion, and common CMS exports, with API publishing options.
